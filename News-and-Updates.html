NATIONAL ID REGISTRATION IN JAIL FOR PERSONNEL AND PDL
10 December 2021

One of the problems suffered by Persons Deprived of Liberty (PDL) in transacting, through their relatives, with both private and government agencies, is the lack of documents to prove their identity. Oftentimes additional documents were required to be presented. With the enactment of RA No 11055, all citizens can now register and secure national ID that can ease or simplify transactions by eliminating the need to present other forms of identification. As such, this jail through JO3 Victor D Santos Jr, Paralegal Officer/HRAO, coordinated with PhilSys Bulacan for national ID registration of both jail personnel and PDL.

On 0800H-1700H 10 December 2021, personnel of PhilSYS Through coordination with Mr Christopher M Rodil, PhilSys Focal Person, conducted national ID registration in this jail. one hundred twenty seven(127) male PDL and five (5) jail personnel. The PhilSys team also accomodated national ID registration of two (2) lawyers from Public Attorney’s Office of PAO Baliwag District Office..
